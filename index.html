Welcome!



I was born in Palma de Mallorca, Spain, in 1983. I received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from the University of the Balearic Islands (UIB), in 2004 and 2007, respectively. In 2011, I presented my master's thesis in order to obtain the master's degree in Information Technology and Communication.

I worked several years in private companies as programmer and consultant. Since November 2011, I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at UIB, with funding from the government of the Balearic Islands. I develop my research within the Systems, Robotics, and Vision group.

My research interests include mobile robotics, topological mapping, appearance-based scene recognition, visual SLAM and unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV's). See my research and publications pages for more information about my research activities.

For further details, please visit my Linkedin or Mendeley profiles.
